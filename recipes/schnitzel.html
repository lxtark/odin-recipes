Schnitzel Recipe

How to make a great Schnitzel

Schnitzel

The best Schnitzel are made from either veal or pork. Best served hot, they can come with many side dishes.

While the breading and meat can be substituted, the process to make Schnitzel is the same.


Ingredients

for 4 servings

  • 2 egg yolk
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • pinch spices
  • 4 pork or veal cutlets

Steps

  1. prehead frying pan to low
  2. combine yolk and milk, whisking into a shallow dish
  3. prepare second dish with bread crumbs
  4. tenderize cutlets to 1/4 to 1/8 inch thickness
  5. dip cutlets into egg/milk mix
  6. dip soaked cutlets into breadcrumbs
  7. lay breaded cutlets into frying pan
  8. cook for 5 minutes per side
  9. take off pan and serve

Serve with Knoedel or other potato dish. Also, add lemon spritz for added flavour.
