Banana Shake Recipe

How to make a great Banana Shake

Banana Shake

A great cool drink for the warm summer, fantastic to chill inside and outside.

Variations are plentiful, however the main ingredients stay the same.


Ingredients

for 4 servings

  • 4 bananas
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 can whipped cream
  • 4 strawberries

Steps

  1. prehead blender
  2. combine bananas, sugar and milk in blender
  3. blend on low for 30 seconds, then on high for 2 minutes
  4. pour into tall glasses
  5. spray whipped cream on top
  6. garnish with strawberries
  7. Enjoy!

A healthy drink for all summer activities!
