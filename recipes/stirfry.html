Stir Fry Recipe

How to make a great Stir Fry

Stir Fry

Enjoyed across the world in many variations, stir fry is a staple to many households. Easy to make, filling to eat and enjoyable by all, the variations of stir fry are what make it great.

With many substitutes available, the basics do not change.


Ingredients

for 4 servings

  • 500g meat
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 packages udon noodles
  • 4 cups veggies
  • pinch Maggie
  • additional flavouring and veggies

Steps

  1. prehead frying pan to low
  2. add meat and cook until browned
  3. add olive oil and noodles, cook for 10 minutes
  4. add veggies and cook until tender
  5. stir and add Maggie and other flavouring
  6. drain any remaining liquid and serve

Generally served on its own, side dishes can include additional meat or flavours.
